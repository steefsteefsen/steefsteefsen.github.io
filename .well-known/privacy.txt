# Privacy stance — „Der springende Punkt"
# Maintainer: Steef <steef@huellinghorst.info>
# Last-Updated: 2026-05-03

Cookies: none required, none set by this site itself.
Tracking: none. No analytics, no pixels, no third-party trackers.
Stalking: not supported. Curiosity is welcome, surveillance is not.

Embeds: SoundCloud, YouTube (via youtube-nocookie.com), Google Fonts.
        Each is a third party with its own policies. The site loads
        their resources only where the visitor sees content from them
        (a SoundCloud embed loads SoundCloud's iframe, not before).

Storage: localStorage is used for visitor settings only — sound on/off,
         easter-egg flag on/off, go-to-top hand preference. No personal
         data, no identifiers.

Reload: cookies and sessionStorage are cleared on every page load.
        localStorage settings persist on purpose, because that's what
        a setting is. Anything else is housekeeping.

Tracking-Free? Yes.
Cookies-Free?  Yes (own domain).
Open-Source?   MIT, mirrored at https://gitlab.com/steefSteefsen/theopenhomepage
