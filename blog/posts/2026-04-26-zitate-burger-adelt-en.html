26 April 2026

quotes, burger, adelt

Two new quotes landed in the hero yesterday: Boris Zubert's "Du bist Mutti." and Einstein's "Peace cannot be kept by force; it can only be achieved by understanding." Einstein and Merkel silhouettes were tried alongside them as faint background artwork — removed in the following commit. The portraits conflicted with the z-index layering for the leaf elements; the quotes stayed, the images didn't.

The mobile hamburger was too thin. Lines are now 26 px wide and 3 px tall, color switched to plain black. The overlay menu swapped inset: 0 for explicit top / left / width: 100vw / height: 100dvh — because backdrop-filter on <nav> creates a new containing block, which makes inset: 0 resolve against the element rather than the viewport. Firefox and Safari Mobile both had the menu fail to fill the screen. They don't anymore.

Peer Adelt joins the idols section, with text in all 17 languages. Short entry, because I can't fully place him yet. He's there until I have more.
