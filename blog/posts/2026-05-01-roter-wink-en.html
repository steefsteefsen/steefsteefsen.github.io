a red flag for may day

A small red flag with a white carnation has been hiding in the hero scroll-hint since last night. Hover twice and the mood shifts — restless instead of gentle. Third time: the escalation caps at stage three. No fourth stage; Pianosa stays controlled. Everything runs in memory: no cookie, no localStorage, no server. Reload and the world is quiet again. A quiet gesture for May Day.

Lÿn, Berlin-based DJ and producer, gets a card in the artist section. No outbound link until consent is formal — that's the Tier-2 rule. The card is there; the link waits.

The footer has lost two links: the open letter to Elon Musk and the one to Collien Fernandes. Both texts are still reachable, just no longer as footer links. The top-level Blog nav link is also gone — the blog lives at /blog/, anyone looking for it will find it.

Two cards were taken offline at direct request. No further comment.
