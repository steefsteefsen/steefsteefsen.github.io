# Open Letter to Achim Watzke — Borussia Dortmund
*Stefan-Olav Hüllinghorst — April 2026*

---

Achim,

I was a BVB fan. Südtribüne. The real kind — not the shirt-on-matchday kind.

Then the club invested in Rheinmetall. A weapons manufacturer. A company that profits from war.

After that, BVB became just another club.

I don't write this with anger. I write it with the clarity of someone who knows exactly when something ended and why.

---

Here is my offer.

Fund my incubator. It's a home for founders with the right attitude — patient, principled, building for something that lasts. Not unicorn-chasers. People who understand that sustainable growth is a foundation, not a constraint.

Cancel the Rheinmetall partnership.

I come back to the Südtribüne.

---

That's it. No lawyers. No press release. Just a fan telling you what it would take.

The Südtribüne is one of the greatest things German football ever produced — 25,000 people standing, singing, believing in something together. It deserves a club that believes in something too.

You still have time to be that club.

Stefan-Olav Hüllinghorst
Berlin, April 2026
