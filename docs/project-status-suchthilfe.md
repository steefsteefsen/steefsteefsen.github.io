# Suchthilfe Platform — Project Status & Roadmap

**Project Owner:** Stefan Hüllinghorst, Berlin
**Contact:** stefan@huellinghorst.info
**Last Updated:** 2026-04-06

---

## Mission

Building a free, digital support platform for people affected by addiction in Germany. Trauma-informed, culturally sensitive, accessible to everyone — regardless of insurance status, income, or background. Funded through grants and institutional support.

---

## Current Status: Early Phase

### ✅ Completed

- **Legal counsel identified:** Top German nonprofit law firm WINHELLER (Frankfurt) contacted for gGmbH formation, Gemeinnützigkeit status, and health data protection (GDPR Article 9). Email sent to info@winheller.com — awaiting response.
- **Creative & communications partner secured:** Undone (Berlin) — the award-winning podcast/documentary production company behind the ZDF Mesut Özil documentary, "Cui Bono", and "Legion" — has agreed to collaborate on the platform's narrative identity, storytelling, and public communications. Key contacts: Khesrau Behroz & Patrick Stegemann (work@undone.work).
- **Domain landscape researched:** Key German domains (suchthilfe.de, suchtberatung.digital, dhs.de) mapped. Strategy favors warm, human naming over clinical terminology. Recommended: .de primary domain paired with .help or .org.
- **Competitive landscape mapped:** Existing digital players include DigiSucht (federally funded, 450+ counseling centers), DHS, Caritas online counseling, DRK Suchtberatung. Gap identified: no platform combining trauma-informed design, cultural sensitivity, anonymity, and zero-barrier access in one modern digital experience.

### 🔄 In Progress

- **Legal structure:** Deciding between gGmbH (€25,000 share capital) and gUG (€1 minimum, convertible later). Awaiting WINHELLER consultation.
- **Funding for Undone collaboration:** €12,000 needed. Active funding tracks:
  - Crowdfunding (Betterplace.org / Startnext)
  - LOTTO-Stiftung Berlin
  - Beisheim Stiftung (rolling applications, focus: health + Berlin)
  - Aktion Mensch Mikroförderung (up to €5,000, no co-funding required — new 2026 calls expected)
  - GKV-Bündnis für Gesundheit (precedent: funds addiction projects in Berlin)
- **Domain selection:** Shortlist under review. Candidates include warm/human names in German and English across .de, .help, .org TLDs.
