Philosophy

What I Believe

I believe the best things grow slowly. Whether cultivating a garden, an idea, or a business — sustainable growth requires patience, deep roots, and an honest relationship with the soil you work in.

"He who plants a tree plants a hope."
— Lucy Larcom

My approach is shaped by the conviction that lasting value comes from understanding systems deeply, nurturing what matters, and building with integrity.

🌱

Cultivation

Treating every venture as a living system — feed it well, give it light, and have the patience to let it grow.

🪨

Sustainability

Building for decades, not quarters. Decisions rooted in long-term health over short-term extraction.

🌎

Connection

Everything is interconnected. The strongest systems are built through trust, transparency, and mutual benefit.

In Development

diedorismachtdasgift.io

A platform offering low-threshold support for people struggling with gambling, alcohol, nicotine, and other substances used in ways that harm themselves or the people around them. Currently looking for beta testers — I am one myself.

The platform will be structured as a gGmbH or gUG — a German nonprofit entity. A funding campaign is in preparation to cover the formation costs (~€12,000), advised by WINHELLER Rechtsanwaltsgesellschaft mbH, Frankfurt am Main, who have proposed to support the company formation, data protection framework, and ongoing compliance. One of the intended funding sources is the Lotto-Stiftung Berlin.

Journey

Where I've Been

The path that shaped my thinking and brought me here.

TU Berlin

Master of Science

I studied mostly among men, having grown up with three older sisters. The contrast was formative. What stayed was something more valuable than the degree: the ability to think scientifically, trust evidence, and question assumptions — including my own.

Three Years

Insolvency

Three years without money. It is not a chapter I am ashamed of — it is one I learned more from than almost anything else. Scarcity teaches you what actually matters and what you were just carrying out of habit.

Mondiali

Die Zwote & the Mondiali Antirazzisti

My team won the Mondiali Antirazzisti in my absence. I am genuinely proud of every person who made that happen. I am also still thinking critically about the role alcohol played in the money we raised for charity — some contradictions deserve to stay open.

Idols

People Who Shaped My Thinking

Not role models to copy — but minds that opened something.

🐧

Linus Torvalds

Built Linux out of stubbornness and precision. Proof that open, patient, unglamorous work changes the world more than any pitch deck.

🎤

Megaloh — der Malocher

German hip-hop with depth, craft, and integrity. He works. No shortcuts, no sellout. The music earns its place.

🎵

Mira — Berlin DJ

For what she builds in a room at night. Aware, precise, present. The kind of artist who knows that context is everything.

✨

Albert Einstein

Curiosity as a life philosophy. He questioned what everyone accepted and trusted the process of thinking over the comfort of certainty.

📚

Sokrates

Knew that knowing nothing is the beginning of wisdom. Never wrote a word — his questions did all the work.

🏭

Platon

Took the questions of his teacher and built a system of thought that still holds weight 2,400 years later. Ideas outlast everything.

👁

Edward Snowden

Gave up everything to tell the truth. Whatever you think of the consequences, the act of choosing conscience over comfort is something I respect deeply.

🌎

Jimmy Wales

Founded Wikipedia — free knowledge for every human being on the planet. One of the most quietly radical acts in the history of the internet.

☮

Dalai Lama

Compassion as a practice, not a feeling. The idea that inner peace and outer responsibility are not in conflict — that you can be both grounded and engaged.
